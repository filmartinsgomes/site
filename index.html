Focus areas

What I write and think about

My work connects academic rigour with practical relevance across these core themes.

⚡ Dynamic Capabilities

How firms sense and seize opportunities and reconfigure resources to maintain competitive advantage in turbulent environments.

🔄 Business Model Innovation

The structures, processes, and logic firms use to create, deliver, and capture value — and how they evolve.

🌐 Innovation Intermediaries

The brokers, platforms, and ecosystems that facilitate knowledge transfer and collaborative innovation between actors.

🧭 Leadership

What it means to lead in complex, uncertain environments — from strategic decision-making to organisational culture.

🔗 Systems Thinking

Understanding organisations and markets as interconnected systems, where structure drives behaviour.
